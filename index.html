Donut Crush

Donut Crush is a Single Player 3D, third person karting mobile game. It has 2 parts. One is standalone, with 3 levels, where the player explores their food preferences like different kinds of donuts and tries to keep up with the different game objectives. The main player exhibits few traits that either helps or jeopardizes their chance in successfully finishing each level. The second part of the game is a race mode. This one has 3 difficulty levels to complete with other AI karts. More the difficulty, more are the gains that the player can bag from it. The player also has the ability to buy new Kart and other look swag by collecting coins from such levels. We also have fun pickups and powers to keep the player engaged during this journey.

The Story

The player loves Donuts and is allergic to anything green! We explore the players food liking through a racing game with different levels and fun tracks. In this process, we also try to instil few healthly eating habits like eating a fruit. We have also offered a Shop for the player to buy new swag from. The player has an opportunity to earn coins either by collecting them or by winning the Race mode levels.
